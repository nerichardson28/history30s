Major Events of the War of 1812

Battle of Queenston Heights

The United States invaded Upper Canada in October 1812 and attacked at Queenston Heights along the Niagara River. British forces led by Major General Isaac Brock tried to stop them, but Brock was killed while leading a counterattack. Later, British troops under Major General Roger Hale Sheaffe and Indigenous warriors defeated the American army. Although the British won the battle, Brock’s death was a major loss, but it inspired Canadians to continue defending their land.
